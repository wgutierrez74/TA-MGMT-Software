TA Hub is a system for prospective teaching assistants and instructors whom are in need of a teaching assistant. Here students can submit their credentials and apply to be considered as a TA for a particular class. Faculty members can then select which students they would like as a potential TA. A perfect fit for both parties is then selected by an administrator and the TA and faculty member are automatically notified.
